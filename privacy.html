Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Overview

Tangent Loop builds apps that respect your privacy. We believe software should work for you without requiring your personal data in return.

Data Collection

Our apps are designed to function without collecting personal information. We do not track you, sell your data, or build advertising profiles.

Specifically, our apps:

  • Do not collect personal information
  • Do not use third-party analytics or tracking services
  • Do not serve advertisements
  • Do not share data with third parties

Local Data

Any data you create within our apps (settings, preferences, content) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

App Store

Our apps are distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain data as described in their privacy policy. This data collection is managed by Apple, not by Tangent Loop.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at han@tangentloop.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
